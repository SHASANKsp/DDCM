  • Selected diseases:
    Selected diseases on the list are made on basis of literature reviews. A significant number of medical research articles have been found under each disease. Till present time 25 genetic diseases have been listed. As we are engaged in constant update of the database, more will be added in a timely manner.

  • Chromosome mutation:

    Fig: Different types of mutation


    Each genetic disease has been listed with their respective change in nucleotide (substitution, inversion, deletion, translocation, duplication) at a specific location on the related chromosome number.

    An allelic mutation on a chromosome has been indicated according to HGVS(Human Genome Variation Soceity) nomenclature.
    Ex:NC_accessionNo.(Chr.No.).VersionNo:g.position(nucleotide substitution).Like, NC_000023.9:g.32317682G>A. Chr. no. = 23, version-9 followed by position and mutation.
    Human Genome Build Version: GRCh38

  • RNA Variants:
    Corresponding RNA variants against an allelic mutation or chromosomal aberation (whichever applied) has been indicated by using ClinVar provided by NCBI.
    RNA variant nomenclature -> NM_accesionNo.Version:c.position(mutation). Ex: NM_000142.5(FGFR3):c.1123G>T(Achondroplasia; FGFR3 gene)

  • Clinical Significance:
    "Pathogenic" or "Likely pathogenic" attributes have been selected against a particular NM ID.

  • Amino Acid substitution:
    For each mutation the respective amino acid substitution has been annotated.
    Example: NP_000133.1:p.Gly375Cys for achondroplasia with substitution from glycine(Gly) to cystine(Cys) at 375th position of polypeptide product.
